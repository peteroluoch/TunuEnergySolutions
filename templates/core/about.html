Our Story

Founded on a vision to transform how the world accesses clean energy.

Tunu Clean Energy emerged from a collaboration between visionary engineers, environmental scientists, and sustainability experts who recognized the untapped potential of micro-hydro power generation. We saw an opportunity to create a solution that could harness energy from even minimal water flows, making clean energy accessible to everyone.

After years of research and development, we perfected our proprietary chipset technology that enables our portable hydro units to generate significant power from small water sources. Today, our solutions are deployed across 30+ countries, providing sustainable energy to communities, businesses, and industries worldwide.

Our Mission

Democratizing access to clean energy while preserving our planet's resources.

We're committed to creating sustainable energy solutions that are accessible to all, regardless of location or infrastructure. By harnessing the power of flowing water in innovative ways, we're reducing global carbon emissions while providing reliable power to those who need it most.

Breakthrough Micro-Hydro Technology

Our revolutionary approach to hydro power generation uses minimal water flow to produce maximum energy output.

Unlike traditional hydro power that requires dams or large water volumes, our proprietary chipset technology and advanced micro-turbines can generate significant power from streams, small rivers, or even industrial water systems with minimal environmental impact.
